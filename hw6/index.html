The Media is a Medium

We take the choices we are given, for there is no other choice —MysteryGirl18

The internet is 2 way, mass communication. But we only have some control over the information we receive, and how we interact with it. For example, we can pause, rewind, or change movies halfway through while streaming. But we cannot change the storyline or color scheme of the movie.

We can post whatever we like. But we can only post what a website allows us to, logistically and semantically. The data will be transmitted as electricity over wires, and we do not have a say in this. We cannot control how various people online interact with our content, how they comment, make memes, post reactions.

Where does the infrastructure of the internet experience end, and the propoganda start? How is the message transformed in the transportation process, and limited by the medium? How is the interaction shaped by the user?

"The eye— it cannot choose but see; we cannot bid the ear be still; our bodies feel, where'er they be, against or with our will." —Wordsworth

In "The Medium is the Massage" by Marshall McLuhan, there are many messages given in variable formats. What kind of message do you want to see? Click a button and explore, for the power of this medium relies on you!
