Web 1.0 vs 2.0

While web 1.0 was much more static and information was "published" to be shared with the public, Web 2.0 marked a new era of websites where users could create + engage with information and share it on the internet with no technical knowledge. People went from creating websites to creating blogs, to posting on social media platforms. In the Web 2.0 era, data became a valuable commodity, and corporations are doing their best to get their hands on it. User interaction (clicks, views, attention) and advertising have become the currency of the internet.
